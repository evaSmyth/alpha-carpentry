About Alpha Carpentry and Renovations

Welcome to Alpha Carpentry and Renovations, a family business rooted in Celbridge, Co. Kildare with over 25 years of combined experience. We're two cousins who are passionate about carpentry and dedicated to providing exceptional service.

At our family-run business, we pride ourselves on mastering the craft of carpentry. From bespoke joinery to intricate custom units, from awe-inspiring media walls to elegant banisters, we infuse each project with passion and precision. Every curve, every joint, every detail is meticulously crafted to exceed expectations and stand the test of time.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. From the initial consultation to the final piece, we collaborate closely with our clients, listening to their vision and bringing it to life with our expertise and ingenuity.

Whether it's a full-scale renovation or a small-scale enhancement, no project is too big or too small for us. We approach each endeavor with the same dedication and craftsmanship, ensuring that every client receives a masterpiece that reflects their unique style and personality.
